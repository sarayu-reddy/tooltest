How to Play?

1. The goal of tic-tac-toe is to be the first player to get three in a row on a 3 x 3 grid.
2. The player who is playing "X" always goes first.
3. Players alternatly place Xs and Os on the board until either one player has three in a row, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally; or all nine squares are filled.

Possible situations

  • Win: If you are able to draw three of your Xs or three of your Os in a row, then you win.
  • Draw: If all nine squares are filled and neither player has three in a row, the game is a draw.
  • Lose: If the opponent player is able to draw three of their Xs or three of their 0s in a row, then you lose.
